DNA Double Helix (NEET Biology)

Deoxyribonucleic acid is a molecule composed of two chains that coil around each other to form a double helix. It carries the genetic instructions for development, functioning, growth, and reproduction of all organisms.

  • Structure: Antiparallel helical strands of nucleotide units
  • Base Pairs: Adenine-Thymine (A-T), Cytosine-Guanine (C-G)
  • Significance: Key building block of life, highly emphasized in genetics

Carbon-60 Fullerene / Buckyball (JEE Chemistry)

Buckminsterfullerene is a spherical fullerene molecule with the formula C60. It has a cage-like fused-ring structure resembling a soccer ball, made of twenty hexagons and twelve pentagons.

  • Hybridization: sp² hybridized carbon atoms
  • Symmetry: Truncated icosahedron (Icosahedral symmetry)
  • Applications: Nanotechnology, drug delivery, superconductors

Atomic Orbitals / Particle Orbit (JEE Physics)

A mathematical function that describes the wave-like behavior of an electron in an atom. This model visualizes the probability distribution of finding an electron in specific regions around the nucleus.

  • Quantum Numbers: Principle (n), Angular (l), Magnetic (m)
  • Duality: Demonstrates the wave-particle duality of electrons
  • Key Focus: Modern Physics, Quantum Mechanics & Atomic Structure
